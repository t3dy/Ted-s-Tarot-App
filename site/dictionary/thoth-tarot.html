Thoth Tarot

Traditions
REVIEWED HIGH SEED_DATA

Aleister Crowley's tarot deck, painted by Lady Frieda Harris (1938-1943).

The most intellectually ambitious tarot deck ever created. Incorporates Golden Dawn correspondences (modified by Crowley), Thelemic philosophy, quantum physics imagery, projective geometry, and alchemical symbolism. Harris's paintings are modernist/abstract expressionist — unprecedented for tarot. The companion text 'The Book of Thoth' was published in 1944 (200 copies). The deck itself was not printed as cards until 1969, after both creators had died.

Significance

The Thoth deck is the philosophical tradition — every card encodes multiple layers of Qabalistic, astrological, and alchemical correspondence. Crowley's keywords are precise and often severe: Oppression, Debauch, Ruin, Cruelty.
