Cartomancy

Divination
REVIEWED HIGH SEED_DATA

Fortune-telling or divination using playing cards or tarot cards.

The practice of using cards for divinatory purposes. The earliest documented tarot cartomancy dates to c. 1750 (an anonymous Italian manuscript discovered by Franco Pratesi). Etteilla (Jean-Baptiste Alliette) coined the term 'cartonomancie' in 1770 and became the first known professional tarot reader. The term encompasses both playing card divination and tarot-specific practices.

Significance

Understanding cartomancy's relatively late emergence (300+ years after tarot's invention as a game) is essential for evidence-based tarot history.
