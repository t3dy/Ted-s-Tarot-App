Structural Reading

Interpretive Method
REVIEWED HIGH SEED_DATA

Reading the overall patterns of a spread (suit prevalence, trump density, number sequences) before interpreting individual cards.

A structural reading examines the composition of the spread as a whole before looking at any single card. Key signals include: suit prevalence (which elemental domain dominates), trump density (personal agency vs. archetypal forces), number sequences (narrative arc through Qabalistic number philosophy), reversal patterns (direct engagement vs. transition/release), and court card density (interpersonal dynamics).

Significance

Structural reading is the meta-layer that most tarot apps and many readers overlook entirely. It provides the interpretive context that shapes how individual cards should be understood.
