Meanings

Rider-Waite-Smith

Reversed

Keywords: Imprudence, incapacity, extravagance.

Upright

Keywords: Skill, bravery, capacity, defence, address, enmity, wrath, war, destruction, opposition, resistance, ruin. There is therefore a sense in which the card signifies death, but it carries this meaning only in its proximity to other cards of fatality.

Thoth / Crowley-Harris

Upright

Keywords: Sun in Virgo; flowering internal and external richness; wisdom, development, carefulness, prudence

Flowering richness both internal and external. Wisdom, careful development, and prudent investment of energy.

Number 8: Intellect

Analysis, communication, attention to detail. The element rationalized.
